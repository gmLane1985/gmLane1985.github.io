As is stated on the homepage "former firefighter/ emergency medical technician". I spent from May 2007-December 2019 as a firefighter, and in between in 2011 I added the role of Emergency Medical Technician to my resume. For as long as I can remember I've always wanted to help others, so working in public safety helped me achieve that. Now after major life events, including the passing of my mother, I find working in public safety doesn't bring me the joy that it always did. So I searched for something new, which turns out to be something old and have found joy once again.

I stated something old because I first discovered computer programming as a freshman in high school, which is where I first started doing anything at all with HTML. I loved it back then, but being the young guy I was, and was seeking adrenaline, I found firefighting was the perfect fit for me. My hope is now, that I can continue to learn new things every single day, whether it be something new in HTML, CSS, JS, Python etc., I never want to stop learning.
